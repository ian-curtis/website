Education

I am currently a senior at Grand Valley State University, majoring in Statistics and French and minoring in Mathematics and Psychology. I also have a Python Developer Certificate from Zero to Mastery Academy.

Following my undergraduate programs, I plan to continue my education with a Master's degree in Data Science & Analytics.

Professional Experience

I currently work part time at ALDI, U.S., where I have been since July 2019. I also work part time for the GVSU Libraries' Open Educational Resources (OER) department as an editorial assistant promoting OER and converting existing textbooks from PDF and Word formats to more accessible online editions (using PreTeXt and Pressbooks).

Further back, I spent two years at an employee at CoreLife Eatery (Team Member and then Lead Grill Cook) and was a local Little League umpire.

Community Experience

I currently serve as the President of the GVSU French Club, Les Francophiles (elected in 2020). I am also a member of the GVSU College of Liberal Arts and Sciences's Student Advisory Committee. Outside of school, I lead a recycling program at the local ALDI store.

In the past, I served as a tour guide (2019-2020) and a Freshman mentor (2020) for the GVSU Honors College. In high school, I was a member of the recycling club for two years and was the president of the school's chapter of the National Honor Society.

Publications and Projects
