Accessibility Note

My goal for this website is that it is able to be read, accessed, and navigated by all users, regardless of device and sight ability. For this reason, I have paid careful attention when designing the site to how other users might see the pages. As of August 2022, the following features are in place:

  • Links are placed inline with explanatory text
  • Images have alt text
  • Abbreviations have titles
  • Proper headings are used
  • The site is responsive (looks good on all screen sizes)
  • Most, if not all, areas can be navigated by a screen reader
  • Links do not open in a new tab
  • A skip to main content button is on every page

That being said, I still have work to do in this regard. I am very open to feedback when it comes to accessibility and am always looking for ways to do better in this regard. Should you come across any problems with the website, whether it be on a certain brower or device or with accessibility, please contact me (an email is slowly in the works, but any social media platform will work).
