About the Track

I'll be honest: I added this one because I wanted the album to have more than 8 tracks but I was itching to release the album and tired of writing new songs. So I messed around on the piano and made a pretty version of Autumn. This was recorded completely by me (through MIDI and electronic instruments) without a metronome. Just some raw music. It makes me even more lost in the autumn forest dream than the original. This was actually the first track I played live (at a high school informal, mini talent showcase thing).
