About the Track

This was my first song with an "untraditional" chord progression and boy was I happy to see that it worked out. I'm very proud of this one. Even though it is pretty simple, especially in terms of melody line, it is milestone in my growth as a musician.

This song reminded me of the summer days when I would be outside playing catch with my dad. So I named it after those days.
