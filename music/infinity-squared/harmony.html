About the Track

This is another example of a track that I wish I could've expanded on, but repeatedly got writer's block. Gah, it drove me nuts never being able to figure out something good to continue the song. It still does. I really like the sound of this one but had to stick with its current length. Its name is a tribute to my attraction towards harmonious chord progression and sounds (like the Four Chord Power Sequence).
