About the Track

The title translates as Thank you my friend where the friend in this case is the French language itself. I have enjoyed learning the language in high school and college and thought that it deserved its own song.

I almost quit French after my two-year requirement for high school. My high school teacher convinced me otherwise (and I thank her for it) and I ended up majoring in French in college.
