About the Track

Sadie is not a person, but a dog. She is a very faithful companion; I can't even explain everything that makes her a great dog. She is always there to greet me each morning and when I come home. She lays with me when I do work from home. We have a connection that is hard to find.

Sadie is a goldendoodle (this is where the blond hair comes from). Please enjoy some of my favorite pictures of her.
