About the Track

The original version of this track was the inspiration for the album as a whole. Some members of my family (and a ton of people in the world) struggle with anxiety/depression and I think it's super important to remember that there is light in the world. Your struggles might never go away completely or at all so find your light. Find those who can support you and always remember that the light never dies.
